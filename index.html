About Us

CLUB MISSION-Promoting STEAM Education: We are dedicated to promoting STEAM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts, and Mathematics) education by integrating robotics and programming into interdisciplinary projects, encouraging creativity and innovation.

CLUB ACTIVITIES-Robot Building and Programming: Organize workshops and sessions where members can learn to build and program robots using platforms like Arduino, Raspberry Pi, or LEGO Mindstorms. Encourage them to design and build their own robotic projects.

LEARNING OPPORTUNITIES-Project-Based Learning: Members work on projects that involve applying their robotics and programming skills. Guidance and mentorship throughout the project lifecycle, allows members to gain practical experience, problem-solving abilities, and project management skills.
